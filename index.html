About Jay's Animal Games 🐾

This is an archive of fun animal-themed games made by animal expert Jay Chung. Website put together by her sister <3

How to Play Connections

Find four groups of four animals that share a common theme.

  • Tap tiles to select them (up to 4).
  • Press Submit to check if they form a group.
  • You have 4 attempts total.
  • Use Shuffle to rearrange the tiles.

How to Play Crossword

Fill in the animal names using the clues provided.

  • Click a cell or clue to select a word.
  • Click the same cell again to switch between Across / Down.
  • Use Autocheck to see correct letters highlighted in green as you type.
  • Without Autocheck, the word highlights green only when fully and correctly filled.
  • Press New Game to get a freshly generated puzzle.
